# LaunchPath: Tracking & Attribution System

## The Core Problem We're Solving

Users send 25 messages with demo links and have no idea:
- Which message actually worked
- Who viewed the demo
- Who filled out the form
- Which prospects are hot vs cold
- What led to closed deals

**Result:** They're flying blind. Can't optimize. Can't prove ROI. Can't scale what works.

### 2. What Gets Tracked

When anyone clicks a demo URL, you capture:

**Basic:**
- Who clicked (prospect ID)
- When they clicked (timestamp)
- Which message they came from
- Which channel (LinkedIn, email, etc.)

**Behavioral:**
- How long they stayed (30 sec vs 4 minutes)
- What sections they viewed
- Did they fill the form?
- Did they click "Book a call"?
- Did they bounce immediately?

**Viral:**
- Did they share it with colleagues?
- How many people from same company viewed?
- Is this an internal buying committee?

### 4. Behavior-Based Lead Scoring

Not all form fills are equal. We score based on buying signals:

**High Score (90-100) = Extremely Hot:**
- Spent 4+ minutes on demo
- Filled entire form (not just email)
- Clicked pricing section
- Clicked "Book a call" button
- Shared with colleagues
- Returned to view again
- Viewed during business hours

**Medium Score (60-89) = Warm:**
- Spent 1-3 minutes
- Filled form partially
- Viewed on mobile
- Single view
- Weekend browsing

**Low Score (0-59) = Cold:**
- Bounced in under 30 seconds
- Incomplete form
- No engagement signals

**Focus your time on the 90+ scores. They convert at 70%+ rates.**
